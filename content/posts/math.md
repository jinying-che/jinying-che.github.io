## Arithmetic progression
$$ S_n = \frac {(a_1 + a_n)n} {2} $$

## Geometric progression 
$$ S_n = \begin{cases}
   \frac {a(1-r^n)} {(1-r)} &\text{if } r \not = 1  \\\\
   an &\text{if } r = 1 
\end{cases}   $$
